# UI Surface Map

This map defines canonical UI surfaces by user role. A **surface** is a coherent product area (portal, dashboard, workflow, or tool) that groups related user tasks.

## Organizer

- Event workspace (event setup, schedule builder, venue details)
- Registration and ticketing console (ticket types, pricing, capacity, promo codes)
- Program and speaker management (sessions, abstracts, speaker assignments)
- Exhibitor and sponsor management (booths, deliverables, approvals)
- Operations dashboard (staff assignments, run-of-show, incident log)
- Reporting and analytics (sales, registrations, attendance, engagement)
- Communications center (email campaigns, announcements, push/send scheduling)

## Attendee

- Discovery and event landing pages (browse/search events, details, agenda preview)
- Registration and checkout flow (ticket selection, add-ons, payment, confirmation)
- My tickets and profile (ticket wallet, personal data, preferences)
- Personal agenda planner (bookmark sessions, conflict warnings, calendar export)
- Event companion surface (maps, notifications, session reminders)
- Networking and messaging (attendee directory controls, meeting requests)
- Support and help center (FAQs, contact support, request status)

## Platform Admin

- Tenant and organization admin (accounts, workspaces, ownership)
- Global configuration (feature flags, system defaults, policy controls)
- Access and identity management (roles, permissions, SSO settings)
- Compliance and audit surface (audit logs, data retention, policy acknowledgments)
- Integrations control panel (API keys, webhooks, third-party connectors)
- Platform health and observability (service status, queue backlogs, alerts)

## Onsite Staff

- Check-in and badge desk (QR scan, lookup, walk-in registration)
- Badge printing and reprint station
- Session room control (capacity tracking, attendance scanning)
- Queue and line management (overflow handling, waitlists)
- Incident and escalation logging (issue capture, handoff to organizer/support)
- Device/tooling setup surface (scanner pairing, printer diagnostics)

## Finance

- Revenue dashboard (gross/net sales, settlements, payment mix)
- Payout and settlement operations (disbursement schedules, reconciliation)
- Refund and chargeback management
- Invoicing and accounts receivable (enterprise billing, payment terms)
- Tax and fee configuration/reporting (jurisdiction logic, filing exports)
- Financial exports (GL mappings, ERP/accounting integrations)

## Support

- Case intake and triage queue (severity, routing, SLA targeting)
- User/account lookup and impersonation controls (policy-gated)
- Order, ticket, and refund assistance tools
- Incident status and known-issues board
- Communication templates and outbound notifications
- Knowledge base and runbook surface

## Exhibitor

- Exhibitor portal home (tasks, deadlines, compliance checklist)
- Company profile and catalog management
- Booth logistics (space details, utilities, shipping forms)
- Lead capture and retrieval tools
- Staff pass and schedule management
- Sponsor deliverables tracker (assets, approval states, placement proof)
- Post-event analytics (lead reports, traffic summary, ROI exports)

## Speaker

- Speaker portal home (session assignments, deadlines)
- Session content management (title, abstract, learning objectives)
- Asset upload surface (slides, handouts, media)
- Availability and scheduling (conflict declaration, rehearsal slots)
- Speaker profile management (bio, headshot, social links)
- Session performance and feedback (ratings, comments, attendance)
- Reimbursement/honorarium workflow (where applicable)
